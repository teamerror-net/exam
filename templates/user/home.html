FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Browse through these FAQs to find answers to commonly asked questions.

To create an account and register for an exam, follow these steps:
* Visit the website and click on the "Login/Register" button.
* Fill in the required information, such as your name, email address, and password.
* Log in to your account.
* Browse the available exams, select the one you want to take, and click "Enroll."

To participate in an online exam, you will need:
* A computer or mobile device with a stable internet connection.
* A supported web browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari).
* Your login credentials (username and password).
* Any specific software or tools required for the exam (if applicable), such as a calculator or a secure exam browser.

Our online examination system utilizes various proctoring methods to ensure exam security. This may include:
* Webcam monitoring to verify the test-taker's identity and monitor for suspicious behavior.
* Browser lockdown to prevent users from accessing external websites during the exam.
* Randomizing question order and shuffling answer choices to deter cheating.
* Timed exams to limit the available time for each question.
* AI-based plagiarism detection to identify copied content.

The ability to review and change answers during an online exam depends on the settings chosen by the exam administrator. Some exams may allow you to review and modify your answers before submitting, while others may not. It's essential to read the instructions provided before starting the exam to understand the rules for that specific test.

If you encounter technical issues during the exam, such as internet connectivity problems or software glitches, follow these steps:
* Try to refresh your browser or restart the exam if possible.
* Contact our technical support team through the provided contact information.
* If you experience a complete disconnection and cannot continue the exam, reach out to support for guidance on how to proceed. They may offer options for rescheduling or troubleshooting the issue.
